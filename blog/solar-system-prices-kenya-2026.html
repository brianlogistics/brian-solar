Average Solar System Prices in Kenya

The cost of installing a solar system in Kenya depends on power requirements, battery capacity and inverter type. Below is an estimated price guide for common solar systems.

Solar System Size Power Output Estimated Cost (KES)
1kW Solar System Small homes / lighting 80,000 – 150,000
3kW Solar System Small homes with appliances 200,000 – 350,000
5kW Solar System Medium homes and businesses 400,000 – 650,000
10kW Solar System Large homes / commercial use 900,000 – 1,500,000

Factors Affecting Solar Installation Cost

  • Solar panel brand and wattage
  • Hybrid inverter capacity
  • Battery storage capacity
  • Installation complexity
  • Mounting structures and wiring

Benefits of Installing Solar in Kenya

  • Reduce electricity bills
  • Reliable power during blackouts
  • Environmentally friendly energy
  • Long-term savings
