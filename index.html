About Brian Solar & Electrical

Brian Solar & Electrical is a trusted solar and electrical services company in Kenya, providing reliable energy solutions for homes, businesses, farms, schools, churches and institutions. We are committed to helping our clients reduce electricity costs, improve energy independence and access clean renewable energy through professionally designed solar power systems.

Our services include solar panel installation, inverter installation, lithium battery systems, solar water heaters, solar water pumps, borehole solarisation, backup power systems, electrical wiring, maintenance and troubleshooting. Every project is carefully designed to meet the specific energy needs and budget of our clients.

With over 5 years of industry experience, we have successfully delivered solar and electrical projects across Kenya. Our team focuses on quality workmanship, safety, customer satisfaction and the use of high-quality equipment from trusted manufacturers.

As a service-area business, we provide site surveys, consultations, system sizing, installation and after-sales support throughout Kenya. Whether you need a residential solar system, commercial installation, agricultural water pumping solution or complete borehole solarisation, Brian Solar & Electrical has the expertise to deliver dependable results.

Our mission is to help Kenyan homes and businesses reduce electricity bills significantly while promoting sustainable and environmentally friendly energy solutions.
